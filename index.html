About Me

Siwmae! That's Welsh for hello! 👋 I am a motion graphic designer from Swansea, Wales. I have a passion for fun, vibrant and challenging graphics work. With 14 years’ experience in the TV industry, I have worked with a wide range of clients and broadcast networks (BBC, S4C, Channel 4 and Sky Vision).

I've created broadcast graphics packages for over 20 TV programmes, including the BAFTA winning Music for Misfits: The Story of Indie and The Prince and the Bomber. I primarily use After Effects for my video work (I'm proficient with the Adobe Suite) and use Cinema 4D for 3D work.

What can I do for you?

Below are just a few ways I can elevate your project. To put it simply, if you can think it, I can create it.

TV Titles and Branding

Opening titles, lower thirds, end of parts and credit sequences. All based around a consistent brand and theme that will unify your TV programme or project.

Animated Maps

Every great factual or documentary video needs a map (I know...I’ve made plenty). Take your viewers to anywhere in the World in various styles and formats.

Information and Data

Graphs and statistical data...all the stuff that usually sounds so bland on paper can be brought to life through graphics and animation. I have a long track record of creating data driven graphics for historic and scientific projects.

Events

Scoreboards, competitor profiles and event layouts. Whether you’re producing a sport, music or cultural event, my events graphics packages will show off your illustrious event.

Compositing and VFX

Ensure your message or scene is presented effectively to the viewer with special effects and compositing techniques. For when camera footage just isn’t enough.

Corporate

Adverts, social videos, explainer videos and tutorials. My graphics will inject your brand and personality into your project, so you can excel at showing your audience how awesome you are.
